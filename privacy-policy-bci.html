Introduction

UBENWA INTELLIGENCE SOLUTIONS INC.   • Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information from our users:

  1. Limited personal information: When you set up Nanni AI, you could be asked several questions including but not limited to your email and the child's name and date of birth. This information is used to enhance our Services and your experience.
  2. Audio Recordings: When you use the App to record your baby's cry, we collect and store the audio recording so that our algorithms can analyze it and provide you with insights.
  3. Pictures uploads: When you generate an avatar in Nanni AI using a picture, we store the AI generated avatar so that we can use it across the app. We do not store the original picture.
  4. Device and Usage Information: We collect information about your device, such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers. We also collect information about your usage of the App, such as the date and time you access the App and the features you use.
  5. Other Information: If you contact us for support or provide feedback, we may collect and store the information you provide, such as your name, email address, and the content of your message.

We may use various analytics tools, such as cookies and similar technologies, to collect and store information about your use of the App. Cookies are small text files placed on your device that help us understand how users interact with the App, enhance your user experience, and tailor our services to your preferences.

You may have the option to set your device to refuse cookies or to notify you when a cookie is being sent. However, some features of the App may not function properly if you choose to disable cookies. By using the App, you consent to our use of analytics tools and cookies in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
